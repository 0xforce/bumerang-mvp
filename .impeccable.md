# Bumerang — Design Context

_Last updated: 2026-03-21. Generated by /teach-impeccable._

---

## Project Overview

Bumerang is a B2B fintech SaaS platform for **cross-border payments**: USD in → USDC on-chain → client SWIFT/WIRE/SPEI out, primarily on the **US → Latin America** corridor. Bumerang takes a revenue cut per payout. The product is in **closed beta** (invite-only). The data model is: Organizations → Members (RBAC) → Virtual Accounts → Deposits → Payouts (with on-chain tx hashes + fee split) + KYC Profiles + Notifications.

## Design Context

### Users

**Founders of SMBs** who are personally doing cross-border payments — not a finance team, not a CFO, not an ops analyst. The founder IS the user. They have high financial stakes and low patience. They open the dashboard to get a fast, clear answer to one question: _"Where is my money and is everything moving?"_ They are sophisticated enough to understand USDC and on-chain settlement, but they want the UI to absorb that complexity for them.

### Brand Personality

**Three words:** Bold. Precise. Kinetic.

Bumerang is the financial layer that moves at the speed of a startup. It is ambitious and direct, not polished and corporate. It earns trust through clarity and speed, not through conservative design. It feels like something built for people who move fast and need their infrastructure to match.

### Emotional Goals

Primary: **Speed & momentum** — the interface should feel like it's already two steps ahead. Transitions are crisp. Data is preloaded. Every action confirms instantly.

Secondary: **Confidence & control** — balances, statuses, and on-chain proofs are always visible. Nothing is hidden behind "contact support."

### Aesthetic Direction

**Reference:** Brex — bold, ambitious, startup energy. High information density without feeling cluttered. Strong typographic hierarchy. Data-forward layouts.

**Anti-reference:** Consumer apps (pastel colors, playful illustrations, informal copy, rounded-everything). Bumerang is not Venmo. It is also not a traditional bank portal (stiff, heavy, form-heavy, serif-dominant).

**Theme:** Both light AND dark mode, fully polished. Light mode is the primary launch experience; dark mode is not an afterthought — it should use the `.dark` tokens already defined (`#0A0A0A` background, `#111111` cards).

**Visual tone:** Monochromatic base (white/near-white cards on cool-gray surface) with surgical use of `#F94212` Energy Core Orange. The orange is the pulse of the system — reserved for live states, critical actions, and active transactions. Everything else is controlled and quiet.

### Design Principles

**1. Velocity First**
Every interaction must feel instant. Skeletons not spinners. Optimistic UI updates. No unnecessary confirmation modals for low-stakes actions. If something takes >300ms, show progress immediately.

**2. The Founder's Command Center**
Key metrics — available balance, pending payouts, recent deposits, any critical alerts — must be visible above the fold on every dashboard load. No scrolling required to understand the current state of the business.

**3. Earned Premium**
Trust is communicated through restraint, not decoration. No illustrations, no gradient blobs, no mascots. Precision typographic hierarchy and careful whitespace earn credibility. The `shadow-ambient` is the only depth cue needed.

**4. Controlled Energy**
`#F94212` is used exclusively for live pulses, active states, and the single most important CTA on a given screen. When everything is orange, nothing is urgent. Reserve it surgically.

**5. Cross-Border Clarity**
Financial figures must be unambiguous at a glance: currency labels always present, `tabular-nums` always applied to amounts, status badges always color-coded (`success`, `pending`, `failed`). USDC amounts and fiat equivalents must always be shown together.

## Component Conventions

- **Tables/Ledger:** No `divide-y`. Row separation via `gap-4`/`gap-6` + `hover:bg-surface`. Amounts always `tabular-nums`.
- **Status Badges:** Use `<Badge>` from Shadcn. Map: `completed/success → emerald`, `pending/processing → amber`, `failed/rejected → red`, `active → orange`.
- **Empty States:** No full-page illustrations. A small icon + one line of `text-slate` copy + one CTA button. Max 3 elements.
- **Data Loading:** Skeleton shimmer (pulse animation), not spinners. Match the exact shape of the content being loaded.
- **Error States:** Inline `rounded-2xl bg-red-50 text-red-600` blocks. Never toast for form errors.
- **Monetary Display:** Always `USDC 1,234.56` format (currency code before number, comma-thousands, 2 decimal places). Fiat equivalents in parentheses: `(≈ $1,234.56 USD)`.
